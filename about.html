About RaapTech

Built by Someone Who Has Actually Run the Floor.

RaapTech is Tim Raap. One operator, one playbook: fix the workflow between your estimators and your shop, using the systems you already own.

Timothy K. Raap

20 years in mechanical contracting. Apprentice to Operations Manager. Local Union 73 certified, 5-year apprenticeship, TAB certified. I ran CAMduct, CADmep, and ESTmep on live jobs for over a decade before charging a consulting fee for any of it.

Then I spent 4 years and more than $50,000 of my own money testing the full estimating and fabrication stack so my clients wouldn't have to. Most of it was noise. A few pieces were gold. RaapTech exists to put the gold pieces in shops that will actually use them — and to skip the expensive mistakes.
