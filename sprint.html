The Three Tracks

Pick one at the audit call. One Sprint, one track.

Track A

RFQ → Qualified Quote

Intake-to-first-response. Email parsing, qualification rules, templated acknowledgments, routing to the right estimator.

Target outcome: first qualified response in under 15 minutes during business hours.

Track B

Estimating → Shop Handoff

The ESTmep / CAMduct / shop-floor pipeline. Takeoff data integrity, spool sheets, cut-list export, labor factors, tablet or paper artifact.

Target outcome: one source of truth from bid to cut, zero re-entry.

Track C

Library & Template Cleanup

ITM libraries, MAJ files, connection and seam libraries, service templates, spec database. Consolidation, naming standards, and a written convention.

Target outcome: new estimator productive in week one, not week six.

FAQ

Does this include AI?

Sometimes. Automation — AI included — is a tool we use where it makes the workflow faster or more accurate. If your bottleneck needs a spreadsheet and a convention change, that's what you get. We pick the fix that works, not the fix that sounds impressive.

Do you need access to our systems?

Yes. Read access to CAMduct / ESTmep, the file shares where libraries live, and the handoff artifact. NDA on request.

Remote or on-site?

Either. Chicago metro includes one on-site day. Outside the metro, travel at cost — most Sprints are fully remote.

What if we want to continue after?

Two paths: another Sprint on a different workflow, or continuity inside RaapTech Skool. We don't push either.

Can you sign an NDA?

Yes.
