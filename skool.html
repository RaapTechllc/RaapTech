How Skool Fits With the Sprint

Sprint = one workflow, two weeks, $4,800 fixed. You bring the urgency; we bring the fix.

Skool = ongoing support, templates, and live problem-solving. You bring the questions; we bring the room.

Both can stand alone. Shops that use both tend to run the Sprint first for the big fix, then settle into Premium for the long tail.
